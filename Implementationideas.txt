Simplest/Cheapest Option - SmartGlasses:
    Item finding:
        -If you lose an item anywhere, the glasses will take into account the location it was last 
        seen. When you ask it, it will tell you were it was.
        - Identify designated important items (such as your phone, house keys, or charger), and record their last known 
        location when they leave your field of view. Once an item reappears, remove the previous location record and 
        update it with the new location.   
        
    Alert emergency contacts:
        - The model will take into account the motion detection of SmartGlasses, and if it detects 
        an individual falling or dizzyness it will alert an emergency contact
        - If the model notices that an individual took medication at a certain time it will keep account of it 
        and if the individual forgets and takes the medication again within a certain time frame it will alert an emergency contact
        for potential overdose.
    Alert individual:
        - An individual can set a reminder for some kind of medication they should be taking at a certain time. If the model sees the
        medication being consumed it will not say anything, but if a set amount of time passes from the when the medication should have 
        been consumed it will send a reminder.
        - Individuals can input their dietary restriction, and if the model sees them consuming 
        something with the ingredient in it, it will alert them.
    


Next-Level Option - HomeCamera:
    Item finding:
        -If you lose an item in your home, you can ask elephant to scan your home and tell you
        were the item is.
    Alert Individuals:
        - If an individual is cooking and forgets something on the stove, the model can use thermal
        vision to determine when it gets too hot and it can alert the individual.
